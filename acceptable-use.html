Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines acceptable use of LeadOS (the "Service"). LeadOS is an AI research workspace that analyzes publicly available business information. By using the Service, you agree to comply with this AUP.

2. Permitted Activities

You may use LeadOS to:

  • Analyze publicly available business information for research and educational purposes.
  • Generate AI summaries and insights about companies you are studying.
  • Organize your own research notes and build a private knowledge base.
  • Compare businesses and understand market context.
  • Ask an AI assistant questions about public business information.

3. Prohibited Activities

You may NOT use LeadOS to:

  • Build or sell marketing lists: The Service may not be used to construct, sell, rent, or distribute marketing lists, contact databases, prospecting lists, or any data product intended for outbound marketing.
  • Unsolicited outreach: The Service may not be used to facilitate unsolicited outbound marketing, cold outreach, cold email, or mass communications to businesses or individuals who have not consented.
  • Scrape private data: The Service may not be used to access private, gated, password-protected, or otherwise non-public information. LeadOS only uses official public APIs and does not bypass authentication, rate limits, or platform protections.
  • Violate platform policies: The Service may not be used in violation of any third-party platform's Terms of Service, Acceptable Use Policy, or rate limits (including Google, YouTube, and any other integrated platform).
  • Harass or harm: The Service may not be used to discriminate against, harass, stalk, or harm any individual or business, or to enable such conduct by others.
  • Resell access: You may not resell, sublicense, or redistribute access to the Service without our written consent.
  • Illegal activity: The Service may not be used for any activity that violates applicable local, national, or international law.

4. Public Data Only

LeadOS exclusively analyzes publicly available business information retrieved through official APIs. We do not access, store, or display private contact information that is not publicly accessible. All business information shown in the workspace is publicly available through the originating platform's official channels.

5. User Responsibility

You are solely responsible for how you use the research and insights generated by LeadOS. If you choose to contact any business you research, you are responsible for complying with all applicable communications laws, anti-spam regulations (including CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and equivalent laws), and the originating platform's contact policies. LeadOS is a research tool, not an outreach tool.

6. Enforcement

We reserve the right to:

  • Suspend or terminate accounts that violate this AUP.
  • Refuse service to any user engaged in prohibited activities.
  • Cooperate with law enforcement or platform operators investigating violations.
  • Update this AUP from time to time as new use cases emerge.

7. Reporting Violations

To report a violation of this AUP, email leadosofficial@gmail.com with details of the suspected violation. We investigate all reports and take appropriate action.

8. Contact

Questions about this AUP? Email leadosofficial@gmail.com.
