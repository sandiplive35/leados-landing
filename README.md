## 🚀 What is LeadOS?

LeadOS is an **AI research assistant** that helps you study publicly available business information. It uses official APIs (Google Maps Places API, YouTube Data API) to retrieve public business profiles, then applies AI to generate summaries, comparisons, and insights — all organized in your private research workspace.

### What LeadOS IS:
- ✅ An AI workspace for business research
- ✅ A knowledge assistant that generates summaries and insights
- ✅ A research organizer with notes and comparisons
- ✅ An AI copilot inside Telegram

### What LeadOS is NOT:
- ❌ Not a lead generation tool
- ❌ Not an email finder or contact database
- ❌ Not a marketing list seller or data broker
- ❌ Not a cold outreach or prospecting platform
- ❌ Does not scrape private or protected information

LeadOS exclusively analyzes **publicly available information** via official APIs and respects all third-party platform policies.
