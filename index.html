Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LeadOS.

What is LeadOS? expand_more

LeadOS is an AI research assistant that helps you analyze publicly available business information. It generates AI summaries, organizes your research notes, and provides insights about companies you're studying — all through a conversational interface inside Telegram.

Does LeadOS analyze publicly available information? expand_more

Yes. LeadOS exclusively analyzes publicly available business information from official APIs (such as Google Maps Places API and YouTube Data API). We do not access private, restricted, or gated information. All data shown in the workspace is publicly accessible.

Does LeadOS sell marketing lists? expand_more

No. LeadOS does not sell, rent, or distribute marketing lists, contact databases, or any form of prospecting data. LeadOS is a research and analysis tool — not a data broker. Users organize their own research within their private workspace.

Does LeadOS scrape private information? expand_more

No. LeadOS does not scrape private or protected information. We use official, publicly documented APIs provided by platforms like Google and YouTube. We respect third-party platform policies, rate limits, and Terms of Service at all times.

Does LeadOS respect third-party platform policies? expand_more

Absolutely. LeadOS only uses official APIs in compliance with each platform's Terms of Service, acceptable use policies, and rate limits. We do not bypass authentication, scrape protected content, or access data that isn't publicly available through official channels.

Is LeadOS an AI research assistant? expand_more

Yes. LeadOS is fundamentally an AI research assistant. The AI helps you understand companies, generates summaries, answers questions about businesses, compares organizations, and organizes your research — like having a knowledgeable research analyst available 24/7 inside Telegram.

How does LeadOS help with business research? expand_more

LeadOS provides an AI workspace where you can study public business profiles, generate AI summaries of companies, take research notes, compare organizations side-by-side, and build a structured knowledge base — all powered by an AI assistant that understands business context.

Is my payment information safe? expand_more

Yes. All payments are processed by Paddle, a PCI-DSS compliant Merchant of Record. LeadOS never sees, touches, or stores your card details. Your payment is protected by industry-standard encryption and 3-D Secure.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? expand_more

Absolutely. Cancel anytime directly from Paddle's customer portal or via the LeadOS workspace. You keep access until the end of your billing period — no questions asked, no cancellation fees. See our Refund Policy for details.

What payment methods do you support? expand_more

Via Paddle we accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Paddle also handles local payment methods and tax compliance globally. All prices are in USD.

Is there a refund policy? expand_more

Yes — if you're not satisfied within the first 7 days of a paid subscription, email leadosofficial@gmail.com with your payment reference for a full refund. After 7 days, cancellations stop the next billing cycle but aren't refunded retroactively. Full details in our Refund Policy.
