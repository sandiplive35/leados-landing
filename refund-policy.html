Refund Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains when and how you can request a refund for LeadOS paid subscriptions (Freelancer and Marketing Pro plans). By subscribing to a paid plan, you agree to the terms below in addition to our Terms of Service.

2. Free Tier

The Free plan does not involve any payment and is not eligible for a refund.

3. First-Time Subscription Refunds

If you are subscribing to a paid plan for the first time and are unsatisfied with the Service, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial payment, provided you have used fewer than 20% of the credits included in that billing cycle. This gives you a fair opportunity to try the Service without risk.

4. Renewal Charges

Recurring renewal charges for subsequent billing periods are not eligible for a refund once the new billing period has started and credits have been issued, except where required by applicable law. We recommend cancelling before your renewal date if you do not intend to continue.

5. Unused Credits

Credits are tied to your active billing period and do not carry a cash value. Unused credits at the end of a billing cycle, or at cancellation, are not refundable and do not roll over unless explicitly stated otherwise for your plan.

6. Billing Errors and Duplicate Charges

If you believe you were charged in error, charged twice for the same billing period, or charged after a successful cancellation, contact us and we will investigate and issue a full refund for any confirmed billing error.

7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email leadosofficial@gmail.com with your Telegram username and the date of the charge, or message us via our Telegram channel. We aim to respond to all refund requests within 5 business days.

8. Approved Refunds

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout (Telegram Payments or our third-party payment provider) and may take up to 10 business days to appear, depending on your payment provider.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

Questions about refunds can be sent to leadosofficial@gmail.com.
