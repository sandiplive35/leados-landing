Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how LeadOS ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the LeadOS AI research workspace and related services (the "Service"). LeadOS is an AI assistant that helps you analyze publicly available business information and organize research. We collect only what is needed to operate the Service and support your account.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Your Telegram user ID, display name, and plan selected.
  • Usage data: Research actions performed, AI queries submitted, notes saved, and workspace activity.
  • Payment metadata: Subscription plan and billing status (processed by Lemon Squeezy — we never see your card details).
  • Public business information: Data retrieved from official public APIs (Google Maps Places API, YouTube Data API) in response to your research queries.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

  • Operate your research workspace and AI assistant.
  • Store your research notes, saved business profiles, and workspace preferences.
  • Process subscription payments and manage your plan.
  • Improve AI summary quality and research features.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.

4. Data From Public Sources

LeadOS retrieves publicly available business information from third-party platforms (such as Google Maps and YouTube) via their official APIs at your request. This data is returned to you through the workspace and is not used by us for any purpose beyond fulfilling your research query. We do not scrape private, gated, or protected information — only data that is publicly accessible through official API channels.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not sell, rent, or distribute marketing lists or contact databases. We may share limited information with:

  • Lemon Squeezy / Razorpay: For payment processing (card details never touch our servers).
  • Google & YouTube APIs: To retrieve public business information you request.
  • Service providers: Hosting (Render) and database infrastructure required to operate the workspace.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect our rights.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information, research notes, and saved business profiles for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing leadosofficial@gmail.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of such a request.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Export your research notes and saved business profiles.
  • Opt out of promotional communications at any time.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS), secure payment processing via PCI-DSS compliant providers, and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at leadosofficial@gmail.com.
